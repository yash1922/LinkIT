LinkIT

Send personalized bulk emails using your own Gmail account and an Excel/CSV contact list.

Prerequisites

  • Create an account (Sign up) and login with the Gmail address you want to send from.
  • Enable 2‑Step Verification on your Google account.
  • Create a Gmail App Password for Mail. See Gmail SMTP Help.
  • Prepare your contacts file:
    • Format: .xlsx or .csv
    • Required column: email
    • Optional columns: any placeholders you plan to use (e.g., first_name, company)
