Enable Gmail SMTP (App Password)

  1. Ensure 2‑Step Verification is enabled on your Google account.
  2. Create an App Password:
    • Go to Google Account → Security.
    • Under "Signing in to Google", open "App passwords".
    • Select "Mail" as the app and "Other" (or your device) for the device.
    • Generate and copy the 16‑character App Password.
  3. Use settings below on the Send page:
    • SMTP Host: smtp.gmail.com
    • SMTP Port: 587
    • Use TLS: checked
    • From: your Gmail address
    • Password: the App Password you generated
Do not use your normal Gmail password. Always use an App Password. Keep it secret and revoke it anytime from your Google Account.
